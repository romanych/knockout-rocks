Bindings
Name: 
<input type="text"
  data-bind="value: name, valueUpdate: 'afterkeydown'"/>
Radius: 
<label data-bind="text: radius() + 'px'"></label>
Color:
Dash: 
<select data-bind="
          value: dash,
          options: [2, 4, 6, 8],
          optionsCaption: 'No dashes'"></select>

Change name, radius or color in corresponding fields
